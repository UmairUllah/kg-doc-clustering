ID: 5640 DATE: Mon Mar 16 15:35:03 PKT 1987 TOPICS: grain barley PLACES: usa cyprus UNKNOWN: C G f3462 reute u f BC-CCC-ACCEPTS-BONUS-BID 03-16 0107 TITLE: CCC ACCEPTS BONUS BID ON BARLEY TO CYRPUS DATELINE: WASHINGTON, March 16 - TEXT: The Commodity Credit Corporation (CCC) has accepted a bid on an export bonus to cover the sale of 8,000 tonnes of barley to Cyprus, the U.S. Agriculture Department said. The barley is for shipment May 5-20 and the bonus of 39.32 dlrs per tonne was made to Harvest States Cooperatives, the department said. The bonus will be paid in the form of commodities from the inventory of CCC stocks. The department said an additional 42,000 tonnes of U.S. barley are still available to Cyprus under the Export Enhancement Program initiative announced on August 26, 1986. Reuter
