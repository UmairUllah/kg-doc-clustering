ID: 20058 DATE: Tue Oct 20 06:20:40 PKT 1987 TOPICS: alum PLACES: uk brazil cuba UNKNOWN: M f0470 reute r f BC-GROUNDED-BRITISH-BAUX 10-20 0060 TITLE: GROUNDED BRITISH BAUXITE VESSEL REFLOATED IN ORINOCO DATELINE: LONDON, Oct 20 - TEXT: The British bulk carrier Envoy, which ran aground in the Orinoco river on October 16, was refloated without lightening on October 19, Lloyds Shipping Intelligence service said. The Envoy, 75,453 tonnes dw, was carrying a cargo of 50,000 tonnes of bauxite from Brazil to Cuba. REUTER
