ID: 13744 DATE: Tue Apr 07 09:34:23 PKT 1987 TOPICS: wheat barley PLACES: spain greece UNKNOWN: G f0783 reute u f BC-SESOSTRIS-SELLS-18,00 04-07 0056 TITLE: SESOSTRIS SELLS 18,000 TONNES BARLEY TO GREECE DATELINE: MADRID, April 7 - TEXT: Sesostris, the Spanish subsidiary of the international grain trader Dreyfus, sold 18,000 tonnes of barley to Greece for delivery from Mediterranean ports from April 14 to 30, a company spokesman said. He gave no details on price. The barley has a 14 pct humidity. Reuter
