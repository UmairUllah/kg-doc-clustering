ID: 13380 DATE: Tue Apr 07 03:36:40 PKT 1987 TOPICS: livestock carcass PLACES: japan UNKNOWN: L f0120 reute r f BC-JAPAN'S-LIPC-TO-BUY-B 04-07 0103 TITLE: JAPAN'S LIPC TO BUY BEEF ON APRIL 23 DATELINE: TOKYO, April 7 - TEXT: Japan's Livestock Industry Promotion Corp (LIPC) said that on April 28 it will import 7,515 tonnes of beef for the first half of the 1987 fiscal year started on April 1 under the simultaneous buy and sell tender system, against 6,813 a year ago. The system calls on the agency to buy beef from trading houses and simultaneously sell it to users in one tender. The tender quota in April-September is 10 pct of the agency's beef import share. Japan's beef import quota in 1987/88 was set at 93,000 tonnes against 85,000 for the same period in 1986/87. REUTER
