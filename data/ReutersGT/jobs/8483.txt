ID: 17633 DATE: Mon Jun 01 12:17:51 PKT 1987 TOPICS: jobs PLACES: portugal UNKNOWN: RM f1634 reute r f BC-PORTUGUESE-UNEMPLOYME 06-01 0068 TITLE: PORTUGUESE UNEMPLOYMENT STEADY IN FIRST QUARTER DATELINE: LISBON, June 1 - TEXT: Unemployment in Portugal held steady at 9.6 pct in the first quarter of 1987 after the same rate in the last quarter of 1986, the National Statistics Institute said. This compared with 11.1 pct unemployment in the first quarter of 1986. The total number of registered unemployed in the first quarter of this year was 437,500. REUTER
