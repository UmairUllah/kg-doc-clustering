ID: 12046 DATE: Wed Apr 01 11:47:47 PKT 1987 TOPICS: iron-steel PLACES: usa UNKNOWN: F f1479 reute u f BC-BETHLEHEM-STEEL- 04-01 0087 TITLE: BETHLEHEM STEEL SETS PLATE PRICE INCREASES DATELINE: BETHLEHEM, Pa., April 1 - TEXT: Bethlehem Steel Corp said its base price for carbon plates and high-strength and low-alloy plates will be increased by 25 dlrs to 405 dlrs a short ton, effective July one. The company said its composite prices for alloy plates will also be increased 25 dlrs per ton on the same date, adding it does not publish its prices for this product. Bethlehem Steel said its composite prices for strip mill plates will be increased 15 dlrs a ton. Reuter
