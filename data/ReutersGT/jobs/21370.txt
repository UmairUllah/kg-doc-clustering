ID: 9812 DATE: Thu Mar 26 08:31:38 PKT 1987 TOPICS: jobs PLACES: france UNKNOWN: RM f0777 reute b f BC-FRENCH-FEBRUARY-UNEMP 03-26 0078 TITLE: FRENCH FEBRUARY UNEMPLOYMENT HITS RECORD 2.65 MLN DATELINE: PARIS, March 26 - TEXT: French unemployment rose to a record seasonally adjusted 2.65 mln in February from 2.61 mln in January and 2.57 mln at the end of last year, the Labour Ministry said. The rise took the percentage of the workforce out of a job to 11.0 pct last month from 10.9 pct in January and 10.7 pct at the end of 1986. In unadjusted terms unemployment fell by around 30,000 last month to 2.70 mln. REUTER
