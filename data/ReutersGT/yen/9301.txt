ID: 18370 DATE: Tue Jun 02 23:39:39 PKT 1987 TOPICS: money-fx dlr yen PLACES: japan UNKNOWN: RM AI f3080 reute b f BC-NAKASONE-SAYS-DOLLAR 06-02 0066 TITLE: NAKASONE SAYS DOLLAR FALL ONLY TEMPORARY DATELINE: TOKYO, June 3 - TEXT: Prime Minister Yasuhiro Nakasone said the dollar's sharp fall against the yen overnight was only temporary. The dollar dropped sharply in New York after news that Paul Volcker would step down as chairman of the U.S. Federal Reserve. Nakasone told reporters he did not expect U.S. Policy to change after Volcker steps down. REUTER
