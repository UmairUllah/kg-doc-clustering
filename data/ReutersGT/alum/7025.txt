ID: 16320 DATE: Mon Apr 13 10:50:19 PKT 1987 TOPICS: acq alum PLACES: usa UNKNOWN: F f1228 reute u f BC-COMMONWEALTH-ALUMINUM 04-13 0106 TITLE: COMMONWEALTH ALUMINUM PUTS PLANT ON THE BLOCK DATELINE: GOLDENDALE, Wash, April 13 - TEXT: Commonwealth Aluminum (Comalco) said it put its Goldendale, Wash., smelter back on the market after would-be buyer, Columbia Aluminum Corp, of Hermiston, Ore., failed to pull together financing by an April one deadline. The plant, which has an asking price of 18.7 mln dlrs plus several mln more dlrs for inventory, it said. Commonwealth said it is continuing talks with Columbia, but has also opened talks with other interested parties. Commonwealth bought the plant in January 1985 and closed it Feb 15, 1987, leaving about 400 workers jobless. Reuter
