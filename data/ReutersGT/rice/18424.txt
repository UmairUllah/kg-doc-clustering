ID: 7160 DATE: Thu Mar 19 11:05:59 PKT 1987 TOPICS: grain rice PLACES: usa UNKNOWN: C G f1215 reute r f BC-ASCS-SEEKS-OFFERS-TO 03-19 0073 TITLE: ASCS SEEKS OFFERS TO PROCESS CCC ROUGH RICE DATELINE: KANSAS CITY, March 19 - TEXT: The Agricultural Stabilization and Conservation Service (ASCS) is seeking offers to process rough rice owned by the Commodity Credit Corporation (CCC) and deliver about 27.0 mln pounds of milled rice for export shipment May 6-20 and May 21-June 5, an ASCS spokesman said. Offers must be received by 1300 CDT April 7, and successful offerors will be notified April 10. Reuter
