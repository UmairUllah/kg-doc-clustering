ID: 11811 DATE: Wed Apr 01 05:01:32 PKT 1987 TOPICS: cocoa PLACES: ghana UNKNOWN: C T f0307 reute u f BC-GHANA-COCOA-PURCHASES 04-01 0072 TITLE: GHANA COCOA PURCHASES SLOW DATELINE: LONDON, April 1 - TEXT: The Ghana Cocoa Board said it purchased 214 tonnes of cocoa in the 24th week, ended March 19, of the 1986/87 main crop season, compared with 456 tonnes the previous week and 372 tonnes in the 24th week ended March 27 of the 1985/86 season. Cumulative purchases so far this season stand at 217,449 tonnes, ahead of the 204,256 tonnes purchased by the 24th week of last season. REUTER
