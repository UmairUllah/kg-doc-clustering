ID: 9915 DATE: Thu Mar 26 10:48:40 PKT 1987 TOPICS: gas PLACES: usa UNKNOWN: F Y f1310 reute r f BC-MOBIL--TO-UPGRAD 03-26 0107 TITLE: MOBIL TO UPGRADE REFINERY UNIT DATELINE: NEW YORK, March 26 - TEXT: Mobil Corp said it will spend over 30 mln dlrs to upgrade a gasoline-producing unit at its Beaumont, Texas, refinery. It said the unit is a catalytic reformer, which converts low-octane components of gasoline into high-octane components for use in Super Unleaded gasoline. The company said the modernization will allow the unit to regenerate catalysts on a continuous basis without shutdown. Currently, it must be shut twice a year. The unit produces 46,000 barrels of gasoline components a year. Construction will start late this year, with completion set for mid-1989. Reuter
