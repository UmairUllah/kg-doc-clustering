ID: 5031 DATE: Fri Mar 13 15:45:35 PKT 1987 TOPICS: livestock carcass PLACES: usa UNKNOWN: F f0317 reute d f BC-U.S.-MEAT-GROUP-TO-FI 03-13 0109 TITLE: U.S. MEAT GROUP TO FILE TRADE COMPLAINTS DATELINE: WASHINGTON, March 13 - TEXT: The American Meat Institute, AME, said it intended to ask the U.S. government to retaliate against a European Community meat inspection requirement. AME President C. Manly Molpus also said the industry would file a petition challenging Korea's ban of U.S. meat products. Molpus told a Senate Agriculture subcommittee that AME and other livestock and farm groups intended to file a petition under Section 301 of the General Agreement on Tariffs and Trade against an EC directive that, effective April 30, will require U.S. meat processing plants to comply fully with EC standards.
