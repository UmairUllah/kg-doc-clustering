ID: 2880 DATE: Fri Mar 06 16:33:49 PKT 1987 TOPICS: gold platinum palladium copper nickel PLACES: canada UNKNOWN: M f0580 reute r f BC-TECHNIGEN-PLATINUM-CO 03-06 0086 TITLE: TECHNIGEN PLATINUM CORP IN METALS FIND DATELINE: VANCOUVER, March 6 - TEXT: Technigen Platinum corp said initial results of a 13-hole drilling program on its R.M. Nicel platinum property in Rouyn-Noranda, Quebec, indicate extensive near-surface zones highly enriched in gold, platinum and palladium. The metals were found in rocks on the periphery of a sulphide deposit. It said values of up to 0.073 ounce of platinum, 0.206 ounce palladium, three pct copper and 4.5 pct nickel were found over a drill section of 13 feet. Reuter
