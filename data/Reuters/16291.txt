ID: 5240 DATE: Mon Mar 16 05:31:50 PKT 1987 TOPICS: ipi PLACES: sweden UNKNOWN: RM f1407 reute u f BC-SWEDISH-INDUSTRIAL-PR 03-16 0073 TITLE: SWEDISH INDUSTRIAL PRODUCTION FALLS IN JANUARY DATELINE: STOCKHOLM, March 16 - TEXT: Sweden's industrial production fell 1.8 pct during January compared with a fall of 0.8 pct the month before and remains unchanged compared with January 1986, according to figures from the Central Bureau of Statistics. The bureau attributed the fall to a long Christmas break and particularly cold weather, which lowered production in the forestry industry. REUTER
