ID: 16100 DATE: Sat Apr 11 07:39:40 PKT 1987 TOPICS: jobs PLACES: spain UNKNOWN: RM f0070 reute r f BC-SPANISH-UNEMPLOYMENT 04-11 0069 TITLE: SPANISH UNEMPLOYMENT FALLS SLIGHTLY IN MARCH DATELINE: MADRID, April 11 - TEXT: Spain's registered unemployment fell by 10,465 people to 2.97 mln or 21.4 pct of the workforce in March, Labour Ministry figures show. Registered unemployment in February was 2.98 mln people, or 21.5 pct of the workforce. The figures were nonetheless higher than those for March 1986 -- 2.8 mln people and 21 pct of the workforce.
