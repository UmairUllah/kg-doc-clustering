ID: 16843 DATE: Fri Apr 17 10:48:31 PKT 1987 TOPICS: ipi PLACES: france UNKNOWN: RM f0258 reute b f BC-FRENCH-INDUSTRIAL-PRO 04-17 0071 TITLE: FRENCH INDUSTRIAL PRODUCTION RISES IN FEBRUARY DATELINE: PARIS, April 17 - TEXT: French industrial production rose a seasonally adjusted three pct in February after an unrevised 1.98 pct fall in January, the National Statistics Institute (INSEE) said. The figure, which excludes construction and public works, put the February index, base 1980, at 102 after 99 in January. INSEE changed its base year to 1980 from 1970 last month. Reuter
