ID: 2217 DATE: Thu Mar 05 12:03:00 PKT 1987 TOPICS: grain wheat barley corn PLACES: france UNKNOWN: C G f0804 reute b f BC-PARIS-TRADE-DETAILS-E 03-05 0090 TITLE: PARIS TRADE DETAILS EC GRAIN TENDER RESULT DATELINE: PARIS, March 5 - TEXT: The EC Commission rejected all bids for free market bread-making and feed wheat and all bids for the special West German tender at today's weekly EC cereals tender, trade sources said here. It granted export licences for 123,000 tonnes of free market barley at a maximum rebate of 138.75 Ecus per tonne and 25,000 tonnes of maize at a maximum rebate of 133.75 Ecus, they said. Licences for 100,000 tonnes of the barley were awarded to French trade houses, they added. Reuter
