ID: 12813 DATE: Fri Apr 03 08:20:03 PKT 1987 TOPICS: cocoa PLACES: netherlands UNKNOWN: C T f0658 reute u f BC-DUTCH-COCOA-BEAN-IMPO 04-03 0078 TITLE: DUTCH COCOA BEAN IMPORTS RISE IN JANUARY DATELINE: ROTTERDAM, April 3 - TEXT: Total Dutch imports of cocoa beans rose to 17,978 tonnes in January from 13,961 in January 1986, while exports fell to 1,852 tonnes from 3,111, the Central Bureau of Statistics said. Cocoa butter imports rose slightly to 1,699 tonnes from 1,507, while exports fell slightly to 6,211 from 6,293 tonnes. Imports of cocoa powder fell to 316 tonnes from 469 and exports to 5,944 from 6,106 tonnes. Reuter
