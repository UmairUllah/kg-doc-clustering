ID: 9326 DATE: Wed Mar 25 10:26:54 PKT 1987 TOPICS: alum PLACES: usa UNKNOWN: C M f1090 reute u f BC-NORANDA-RAISES-PRIMAR 03-25 0059 TITLE: NORANDA RAISES PRIMARY ALUMINUM PRICES DATELINE: New York, March 25 - TEXT: Noranda Aluminum Inc. said it has increased its primary aluminum prices by two cents a lb, effective with new orders as of March 25 and all shipments beginning May 1. The new price for unalloyed ingot will be 64.5 cents a lb while the new price for extrusion billet will be 72.5 cents a lb. Reuter
