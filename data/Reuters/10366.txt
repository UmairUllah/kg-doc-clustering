ID: 19329 DATE: Fri Jun 19 14:05:31 PKT 1987 TOPICS: alum PLACES: usa UNKNOWN: M F f1918 reute u f BC-alcan-aluminum-price 06-19 0053 TITLE: ALCAN RAISES ALUMINUM PRICES TWO CENTS A LB DATELINE: NEW YORK, June 19 - TEXT: Alcan Aluminum Corp, a subsidiary of Alcan Aluminium Ltd, said it increased its U.S. primary aluminum prices by two cents a lb, effective yesterday. The new prices are 72 cents a lb for ingot and 80 cents for extrusion billet, the company said. Reuter
