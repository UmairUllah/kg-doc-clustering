ID: 18367 DATE: Tue Jun 02 22:58:37 PKT 1987 TOPICS: gas PLACES: singapore UNKNOWN: Y f3058 reute u f BC-ESS0-LOWERS-PREMIUM-P 06-02 0063 TITLE: ESS0 LOWERS PREMIUM PETROL PUMP PRICE DATELINE: SINGAPORE, June 3 - TEXT: Esso Singapore Pte Ltd will lower the pump price of its premium 97 octane petrol price to 96.8 cents/litre from 97 cents effective midnight tonight, a spokeswoman said. The new price is similar to other oil companies' price for the same grade. Esso's price for 95 octane remains unchanged at 90.8 cents. REUTER
