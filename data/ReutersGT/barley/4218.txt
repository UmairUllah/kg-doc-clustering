ID: 13795 DATE: Tue Apr 07 09:55:04 PKT 1987 TOPICS: grain barley PLACES: spain saudi-arabia UNKNOWN: C G f0851 reute u f BC-SPAIN-TO-SELL-BARLEY 04-07 0052 TITLE: SPAIN TO SELL BARLEY TO SAUDI ARABIA DATELINE: MADRID, April 7 - TEXT: Spain will shortly sign with Saudi Arabia an order for barley for April/May delivery, trade sources said. They gave no details on amounts or prices, but said it would be a "major" order. Saudi Arabia bought 500,000 tonnes of Spanish barley last year. Reuter
