ID: 15623 DATE: Thu Apr 09 11:10:12 PKT 1987 TOPICS: iron-steel PLACES: canada UNKNOWN: E f1271 reute u f BC-CANAM-MANAC-WINS-8.5 04-09 0062 TITLE: CANAM MANAC WINS 8.5 MLN DLR CONTRACT DATELINE: MONTREAL, April 9 - TEXT: (The Canam Manac Group Inc) said its Canam Steel Works unit received a contract valued at 8.5 mln dlrs to supply steel trusses to (Canron Inc) for a new car plant in Ingersoll, Ontario. Canam Manac said it will produce the trusses at its Quebec and Ontario plants and delivery will be completed by August. Reuter
