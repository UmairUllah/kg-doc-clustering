ID: 8978 DATE: Tue Mar 24 15:14:04 PKT 1987 TOPICS: cocoa PLACES: uk haiti czechoslovakia UNKNOWN: C T f2257 reute d f BC-HAITI,-CZECHOSLOVAKIA 03-24 0073 TITLE: HAITI, CZECHOSLOVAKIA JOIN COCOA ORGANIZATION DATELINE: LONDON, March 24 - TEXT: Haiti and Czechoslovakia have joined the International Cocoa Organization (ICCO), bringing membership in the United Nations charter body to 18 importing countries and 17 exporters, ICCO officials said. Haiti has provisionally applied to the ICCO as an exporting member, and accounts for 0.92 pct of world cocoa exports, they said. Czechoslovakia joined as an importer. Reuter
