ID: 12621 DATE: Thu Apr 02 11:42:34 PKT 1987 TOPICS: iron-steel PLACES: usa UNKNOWN: F f1541 reute u f BC-BETHLEHEM-STEEL- 04-02 0063 TITLE: BETHLEHEM STEEL RAISES H-PILE PRICES DATELINE: BETHLEHEM, Pa., April 2 - TEXT: Bethlehem Steel Corp said effective with shipments of May 3 transaction prices for all H-piles will be increased by 30 dlrs per short ton. The company said a new base price, coupled with revised size extras, will result in a published price of 446 dlrs per ton HP14 and 420 dlrs for all other H-pile sizes. Reuter
