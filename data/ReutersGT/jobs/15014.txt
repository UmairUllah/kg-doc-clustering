ID: 4091 DATE: Thu Mar 12 05:22:14 PKT 1987 TOPICS: jobs PLACES: denmark UNKNOWN: RM f0391 reute r f BC-DANISH-UNEMPLOYMENT-R 03-12 0062 TITLE: DANISH UNEMPLOYMENT RISES TO 7.9 PCT IN JANUARY DATELINE: COPENHAGEN, March 12 - TEXT: Denmark's seasonally adjusted unemployment rate rose to 7.9 pct of the workforce in January from 7.8 pct in December, unchanged from January 1986, the National Statistics Office said. The total of unemployed in January was 216,200 against 212,200 in December and 216,600 in January 1986. REUTER
