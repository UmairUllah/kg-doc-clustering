ID: 9014 DATE: Tue Mar 24 16:09:09 PKT 1987 TOPICS: iron-steel PLACES: usa UNKNOWN: F f2445 reute u f BC-USX--USS-UNIT-RAIS 03-24 0068 TITLE: USX USS UNIT RAISES PRICES DATELINE: LORAIN, Ohio, March 24 - TEXT: USX Corp's USS subsidiary said that effective with shipments beginning July 1 prices for all leaded grades and 1200-series grades of hot rolled bar and semi-finished products from its Lorain, Ohio, facility will be increased by 15 dlrs a ton over the prices in effect June 1. It said the increase is being made to reflect current market conditions. Reuter
