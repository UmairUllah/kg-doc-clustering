ID: 450 DATE: Mon Mar 02 10:39:16 PKT 1987 TOPICS: ipi PLACES: canada UNKNOWN: E A RM f0730 reute u f BC-CANADA-INDUSTRIAL-PRI 03-02 0085 TITLE: CANADA INDUSTRIAL PRICES UP 0.2 PCT IN MONTH DATELINE: OTTAWA, March 2 - TEXT: Canada's industrial product price index rose 0.2 pct in January after falling 0.2 pct in each of the two previous months, Statistics Canada said. The rise was led by price gains for papers, pharmaceuticals and petroleum and coal products. Price declines were recorded for meat products, lumber and motor vehicles. On a year over year basis, the federal agency said the index fell 0.9 pct in January, the largest yearly decline on record. Reuter
