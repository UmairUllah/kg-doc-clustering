ID: 10619 DATE: Sat Mar 28 06:23:26 PKT 1987 TOPICS: cocoa PLACES: uk japan UNKNOWN: T C f2758 reute r f BC-JAPAN-JOINING-INTERNA 03-28 0067 TITLE: JAPAN JOINING INTERNATIONAL COCOA AGREEMENT DATELINE: LONDON, March 28 - TEXT: Japan is in the process of joining the International Cocoa Organisation (ICCO), which will bring the number of members in the body to 36, ICCO officials said. Japan is completing constitutional procedures necessary to its accession to the ICCO and is expected to become an importing member in three to four months, they said. REUTER
