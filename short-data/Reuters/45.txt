ID: 10038 DATE: Thu Mar 26 12:50:57 PKT 1987 TOPICS: gas PLACES: usa UNKNOWN: F Y f1896 reute r f BC-corrected---mobil 03-26 0119 TITLE: (CORRECTED) - MOBIL TO UPGRADE REFINERY UNIT DATELINE: NEW YORK, March 26 - TEXT: Mobil Corp said it will spend over 30 mln dlrs to upgrade a gasoline-producing unit at its Beaumont, Texas, refinery. It said the unit is a catalytic reformer, which converts low-octane components of gasoline into high-octane components for use in Super Unleaded gasoline. The company said the modernization will allow the unit to regenerate catalysts on a continuous basis without shutdown. Currently, it must be shut twice a year. The unit produces 46,000 barrels of gasoline components a day. Construction will start late this year, with completion set for mid-1989.
