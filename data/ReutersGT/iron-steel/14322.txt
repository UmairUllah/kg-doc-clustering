ID: 3469 DATE: Wed Mar 11 04:15:45 PKT 1987 TOPICS: iron-steel PLACES: usa japan UNKNOWN: F M C f0312 reute u f BC-NIPPON-STEEL,-INLAND 03-11 0082 TITLE: NIPPON STEEL, INLAND DISCUSSING U.S. JOINT VENTURE DATELINE: TOKYO, March 11 - TEXT: Nippon Steel Corp and of the U.S. Are negotiating to set up a joint steel venture in Indiana, a Nippon Steel spokesman said, declining to give more details. Several local newspapers reported the joint venture would be capitalised at 150 mln dlrs, owned 60 pct by Inland and 40 pct by Nippon Steel, and have annual production capacity of one mln tonnes of steel to supply car makers.
