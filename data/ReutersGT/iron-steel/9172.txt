ID: 18254 DATE: Tue Jun 02 15:37:56 PKT 1987 TOPICS: iron-steel PLACES: usa UNKNOWN: F f2479 reute u f BC-USX--UNIT-RAISES-P 06-02 0077 TITLE: USX UNIT RAISES PRICES ON CERTAIN GRADES DATELINE: LORAIN, Ohio, June 2 - TEXT: USX Corp's USS division said it was raising prices for all hot rolled bar and semi-finished products 1100 series grades by 10 dlrs per ton effective July One, 1987. The company said the increase reflects current market conditions. The company could not say what percentage the increase reflects from current prices, nor could it say how much per ton the products sell for currently. Reuter
