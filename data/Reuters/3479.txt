ID: 13129 DATE: Fri Apr 03 16:08:37 PKT 1987 TOPICS: grain rice PLACES: usa turkey UNKNOWN: C G f2237 reute u f BC-TURKEY-ELIGIBLE-FOR-U 04-03 0074 TITLE: TURKEY ELIGIBLE FOR U.S. BONUS RICE DATELINE: WASHINGTON, April 3 - TEXT: The U.S. Agriculture Department said Turkey has been made eligible for the sale of up to 70,000 tonnes of medium grain milled rice under the department's export enhancement program, EEP. As with the 65 previous EEP initiatives, the export sales would be subsidized with commodities from the inventory of the Commodity Credit Corp and made at competitive world prices, USDA said. Reuter
