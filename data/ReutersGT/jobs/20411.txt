ID: 895 DATE: Tue Mar 03 03:27:02 PKT 1987 TOPICS: jobs PLACES: finland UNKNOWN: RM f0610 reute r f BC-FINNISH-UNEMPLOYMENT 03-03 0057 TITLE: FINNISH UNEMPLOYMENT AT 6.7 PCT IN DECEMBER DATELINE: HELSINKI, March 3 - TEXT: Finnish unemployment was 6.7 pct in December last year compared with 6.8 pct in November and 6.1 pct in December 1985, the Central Statistical Office said. It said 173,000 people were unemployed in December 1986, 174,000 in November and 157,000 in December 1985. REUTER
