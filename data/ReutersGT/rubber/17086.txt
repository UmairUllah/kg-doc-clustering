ID: 5957 DATE: Tue Mar 17 10:18:55 PKT 1987 TOPICS: rubber PLACES: switzerland UNKNOWN: T f5079 reute u f BC-NEGOTIATORS-DRAFT-DET 03-17 0088 TITLE: NEGOTIATORS DRAFT DETAILS OF NEXT RUBBER PACT DATELINE: GENEVA, March 17 - TEXT: Rubber producers and consumers, who agreed last week on the central elements of a new international natural rubber pact, have started work on the legal drafting of a future accord, delegates said. Compromise on issues blocking an agreement was reached at a United Nations conference on an accord to replace the current pact, which expires in October. The new International Natural Rubber Agreement (INRA) is expected to be formally adopted on Friday. Reuter
