ID: 10615 DATE: Sat Mar 28 04:18:48 PKT 1987 TOPICS: rubber PLACES: malaysia UNKNOWN: T C f2739 reute r f BC-MALAYSIA-EXPECTS-RUBB 03-28 0094 TITLE: MALAYSIA EXPECTS RUBBER SHORTAGE IN APRIL/MAY DATELINE: KUALA LUMPUR, March 28 - TEXT: Malaysia said it expects a natural rubber shortage in April and May because of the effects of current wintering on rubber trees. The expected shortage and stronger demand for rubber goods, especially condoms and surgical gloves by consumers, is likely to push up prices, the Malaysian Rubber Exchange and Licensing Board said in its latest monthly bulletin. During the annual wintering period from February to April rubber trees shed their leaves and latex output is very low. REUTER
