ID: 12470 DATE: Thu Apr 02 07:19:18 PKT 1987 TOPICS: money-fx dlr yen interest PLACES: japan UNKNOWN: A f0638 reute u f BC-BANK-OF-JAPAN-INTERVE 04-02 0081 TITLE: BANK OF JAPAN INTERVENES JUST AFTER TOKYO OPENING DATELINE: TOKYO, April 2 - TEXT: The Bank of Japan intervened just after the Tokyo market opened, buying dollars at around 147.65 yen, dealers said. They were unsure of the amount of the central bank's purchasing, but it seemed to prevent the dollar from weakening against the yen amid bearish sentiment for the U.S. Currency, they said. The dollar opened at 147.65 yen against 147.20/30 in New York and 146.90 at the close here yesterday. REUTER
