ID: 5435 DATE: Mon Mar 16 11:42:32 PKT 1987 TOPICS: acq copper PLACES: west-germany UNKNOWN: C M f2554 reute r f BC-MIM-TO-ACQUIRE-STAKE 03-16 0108 TITLE: MIM TO ACQUIRE STAKE IN GERMAN COPPER PRODUCER DATELINE: FRANKFURT, March 16 - TEXT: Mount Isa Mines Holding Ltd plans to acquire a 30 pct stake in Europe's largest primary copper producer, Norddeutsche Affinerie AG, a spokesman for Metallgesellschaft AG said. MIM intends to take Preussag AG's total 20 pct share in the copper producer in exchange for some three pct of MIM's share capital. MIM will also take another 10 pct now held by Degussa AG, reducing Degussa's share to 30 pct from 40. Metallgesellschaft's share will remain at 40 pct. The move is subject to approval of the federal cartel office and supervisory boards of the companies involved. Reuter
