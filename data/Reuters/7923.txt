ID: 17129 DATE: Fri Apr 24 12:36:47 PKT 1987 TOPICS: ipi PLACES: usa UNKNOWN: F A RM f1702 reute r f BC-U.S.-PRODUCTIVITY-REP 04-24 0065 TITLE: U.S. PRODUCTIVITY REPORT RESCHEDULED TO MAY 4 DATELINE: WASHINGTON, April 24 - TEXT: The Labor Department said it rescheduled the release of the first-quarter nonfarm productivity report to May 4 at 1000 EDT (1400 GMT) from April 27. The delay was necessary because the Commerce Department moved back release of national income and product data used by the Labor Department to compute productivity. Reuter
