ID: 13294 DATE: Tue Apr 07 00:07:06 PKT 1987 TOPICS: grain rice PLACES: thailand UNKNOWN: G C f0002 reute u f BC-THAI-RICE-EXPORTS-FAL 04-07 0097 TITLE: THAI RICE EXPORTS FALL IN WEEK TO MARCH 31 DATELINE: BANGKOK, April 7 - TEXT: Thailand exported 75,160 tonnes of rice in the week ended March 31, down from 88,785 tonnes the previous week, the Commerce Ministry said. It said the government and private exporters shipped 36,552 and 38,608 tonnes respectively. Private exporters concluded advance weekly sales for 22,086 tonnes against 44,483 tonnes the previous week. Thailand exported 1.23 mln tonnes of rice in January/March, down from 1.29 mln tonnes a year ago. It has commitments to export another 381,171 tonnes this year. REUTER
