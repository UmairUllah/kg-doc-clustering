ID: 16776 DATE: Fri Apr 17 05:27:56 PKT 1987 TOPICS: rubber PLACES: thailand china UNKNOWN: T C f0102 reute u f BC-THAI-VENTURE-WILL-SEL 04-17 0091 TITLE: THAI VENTURE WILL SELL RUBBER TO CHINA DATELINE: BANGKOK, April 17 - TEXT: Teck Soon Co Ltd, a major Thai rubber exporter has formed a joint venture with state-owned Chinese International Economic and Technology Development Corp to produce 50,000 tonnes of sheet rubber annually for export to the Chinese auto industry, Teck Soon general manager Chit Surivitchpan said. Chit said a new joint venture company will have a registered capital of four mln dlrs. China imported 69,952 tonnes of Thai sheet rubber last year and 60,296 tonnes in 1985. REUTER
