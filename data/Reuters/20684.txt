ID: 9195 DATE: Wed Mar 25 06:19:48 PKT 1987 TOPICS: silver copper PLACES: west-germany UNKNOWN: RM C M f0405 reute r f BC-WEST-GERMANS-TO-MINT 03-25 0054 TITLE: WEST GERMANS TO MINT COMMUNITY ANNIVERSARY COIN DATELINE: BONN, March 25 - TEXT: The West German Cabinet approved a plan to mint a special 10-mark coin commemorating the 30th anniversary of the European Community this year. The silver-copper alloy coin will be minted in an edition of 8.35 mln, a Finance Ministry statement said. REUTER
