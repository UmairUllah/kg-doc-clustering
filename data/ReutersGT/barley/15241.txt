ID: 4296 DATE: Thu Mar 12 11:44:14 PKT 1987 TOPICS: grain wheat barley corn PLACES: belgium UNKNOWN: C G f0496 reute b f BC-EC-COMMISSION-GRANTS 03-12 0101 TITLE: EC COMMISSION GRANTS EXPORT LICENCES - TRADE DATELINE: BRUSSELS, March 12 - TEXT: The European Community's, EC, cereal management committee granted export licences for 5,000 tonnes of quality soft bread-making wheat at a maximum export rebate of 134.75 European currency units, Ecus, per tonne, traders said. The committee also granted export certificates of 35,000 tonnes of barley at 137.35 Ecus per tonne, but rejected all bids for the export of soft feed wheat, they said. Certificates were also granted for the export of 15,000 tonnes of maize at a maximum rebate of 132.90 Ecus per tonne, the traders said. Reuter
