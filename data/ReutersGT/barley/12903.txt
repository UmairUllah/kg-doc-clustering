ID: 2191 DATE: Thu Mar 05 11:42:35 PKT 1987 TOPICS: grain wheat barley PLACES: belgium UNKNOWN: C G f0705 reute b f BC-EC-REJECTS-ALL-WHEAT 03-05 0057 TITLE: EC REJECTS WHEAT EXPORT BIDS, GRANTS BARLEY DATELINE: BRUSSELS, March 5 - TEXT: The European Community's cereal management committee rejected all bids to export free market soft wheat at today's weekly tender, traders said. The committee awarded 123,000 tonnes of free market barley export licences at a maximum export refund of 138.75 Ecus per tonne.
