ID: 15676 DATE: Thu Apr 09 11:51:04 PKT 1987 TOPICS: grain barley corn wheat PLACES: UNKNOWN: C G f1455 reute b f BC-EC-GRANTS-FREE-MARKET 04-09 0059 TITLE: EC GRANTS FREE MARKET BARLEY, MAIZE EXPORTS DATELINE: LONDON, April 9 - TEXT: The European Commission authorised the export of 65,000 tonnes of free market barley at today's tender at a maximum rebate of 138.75 European currency units and 55,000 tonnes of French maize at 130 Ecus, grain traders here said. It rejected bids for breadmaking and feed wheat, they said. Reuter
